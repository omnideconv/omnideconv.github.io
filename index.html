omnideconv

Enabling the deconvolution of any cell type, tissue, and organism

Welcome to omnideconv!

omnideconv is an ecosystem of user-friendly tools and resources for cell-type deconvolution.

The omnideconv ecosystem has been developed in a joint effort of the Group of Computational Biomedicine (University of Innsbruck, Austria) and the Group of Data Science in Systems Biology (TUM School of Life Sciences, Germany).
